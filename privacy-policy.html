# Privacy Policy for TattooDay **Effective Date:** September 29, 2025 **Last Updated:** September 29, 2025 ## Introduction TattooDay ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. ## Information We Collect ### Personal Information - **Account Information:** Username, email address, profile information - **Location Data:** City and country for artist discovery and location-based features - **Profile Content:** Profile photos, tattoo images, and posts you create ### Device Permissions #### Camera Permission We request camera access to: - Take photos for your profile picture - Capture images of tattoos for posts - Share visual content with other users #### Photo Library Access We access your photo library to: - Select existing images for your profile - Choose photos to share in posts - Save images to your device when desired #### Location Access We use location services to: - Help users find tattoo artists in their area - Display location-based content and recommendations - Show artist availability in your region #### Notification Permission We send notifications for: - New messages from other users - Likes and comments on your posts - Artist availability updates - Contest and community updates #### Storage Access We access device storage to: - Cache images for better performance - Store user preferences locally - Manage temporary files ## How We Use Your Information - **Service Delivery:** Provide core app functionality including user profiles, messaging, and content sharing - **Communication:** Send notifications about app activity and updates - **Safety & Security:** Protect against fraud and ensure platform safety - **Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve our services ## Data Sharing We do not sell your personal information. We may share data: - **With Other Users:** Profile information and posts you choose to make public - **Service Providers:** Third-party services that help operate our app (Firebase, cloud storage) - **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect rights and safety ## Firebase Integration Our app uses Google Firebase services for: - **Authentication:** Secure login and account management - **Database:** Storing user profiles and app content - **Cloud Storage:** Hosting images and media files - **Analytics:** Understanding app usage (anonymized data) - **Crash Reporting:** Identifying and fixing technical issues Firebase operates under Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy ## Your Rights You have the right to: - **Access:** Request copies of your personal data - **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and associated data - **Correct:** Update or correct your information - **Export:** Download your data in a portable format ## Data Security We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information: - Encrypted data transmission - Secure cloud storage - Regular security updates - Access controls and authentication ## Data Retention We retain your information: - **Active Accounts:** While your account remains active - **Deleted Accounts:** Up to 30 days after deletion for recovery purposes - **Legal Requirements:** As required by applicable law ## Children's Privacy Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. ## International Data Transfers Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers. ## Changes to Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email. ## Contact Information If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at: **Email:** tattoodayapp1@gmail.com **App:** TattooDay Support Section ## Permissions Summary The following permissions are used in our app: - `android.permission.CAMERA` - Take photos for profiles and posts - `android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` - Access photo library - `android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` - Save images to device - `android.permission.ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION` - Location data from images - `android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO` - Audio recording features - `android.permission.INTERNET` - Network connectivity - `android.permission.VIBRATE` - Notification feedback All permissions are requested only when needed and with user consent. --- _This policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play Store requirements._
